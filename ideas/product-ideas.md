# Product Ideas

## Idea A: Watchlist Notes

Researchers want a lightweight way to attach notes to symbols in a watchlist.

## Idea B: Data Import Status

Developers want visibility into whether local market-data imports succeeded, failed, or produced partial data.

## Idea C: Anomaly Review Queue

Researchers want a queue of detected data anomalies that can be reviewed without mutating the raw imported data.

## Idea D: Session Calendar

The system should understand market sessions well enough to distinguish real overnight gaps from missing data.
